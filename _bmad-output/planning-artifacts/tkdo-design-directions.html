My Recommendations for tkdo

Based on your goals (Marie-Claire usability, mobile-first, WhatsApp-like simplicity):

  • Navigation: A (Bottom Tabs + Chips) - familiar, scalable, works on mobile
  • Cards: B (Detailed) or D (Expandable) - gift-givers need context to decide
  • Add Flow: A (Inline) with B (Modal) for "add more details" - progressive disclosure
